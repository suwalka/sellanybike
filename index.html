Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does it work?
    Enter Make, Model and Year of your bike. Provide us basic information about your bike. We will evaluate your bike and provide you within an initial market value of your bike. As you can imagine it is not possible to asses a bike properly without having examined it ohysically. There fore we will book an appointment with you to examine your bike and onsite inspection of vehicle before we offer to buy it. This service is completely free. Then we will make you a fair offer.
  • How do I make appointment?
    If you think the online valuation is satisfactory, you can easily book your individual appointment at your home or office. Make sure you bring all documents, two sets of keys and the service book with you.
  • How do you calculate the price for my bike?
    Our experience in bike buying as well as our comprehensive market knowledge allow us to evaluate bikes realistically and with concern to the current market situation.
  • What documents are required?
    • Aadhar Card or any other government issued id.
    • Bike RC
    • Finance Details
    • Bank details of an account in the name of the registered owner
    • Service Book
  • Why do you need my email?
    We need your email so that we can send you the confirmation of your bike valuation and appointment details. We fully respect your privacy.
  • How do I unsubscribe from email?
    Please use the unsubscribe link in the email.
